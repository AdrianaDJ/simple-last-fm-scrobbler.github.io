Simple Last.fm Scrobbler

Share music meta-data from your Android phone!

Key Features
  • Support for Last.fm, Libre.fm, and ListenBrainz
  • Offline storage of track meta data
  • Plenty of music apps supported
  • Built-in track metadata filter
  • Now playing notifications
  • Dark and Light themes
Support Phones

Any Android device that can install and run Simple Last.fm Scrobbler should generally work.
